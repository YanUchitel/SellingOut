Nintendo DS - It's for everyone!

The Nintendo DS is a handheld game console developed and produced by Nintendo. It was released in 2004 and was the first console in the Nintendo DS line. The Nintendo DS features a clamshell design with two screens, one of which is a touchscreen. The console also features wireless connectivity, allowing players to connect and play with each other over a local network.

Games

The Nintendo DS has an extensive library of games, with titles from many different genres. Some of the most popular games for the console include:

  • Animal Crossing: Wild World
  • Mario Kart DS
  • The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass
  • New Super Mario Bros.
  • Pokemon Diamond and Pearl
